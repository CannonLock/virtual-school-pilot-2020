OSG-VSP’20 – Staff¶

Tim Cartwright

Tim founded the OSG School in 2010 and has led it since. When not involved in training, he mostly works for OSG in management, where he helps the project as a whole in a variety of capacities. Tim started working with OSG in 2005, originally focusing on software integration.

Lauren Michael

Lauren serves as co-Director of the OSG School program. She leads Research Computing Facilitation teams for the OSG and at UW-Madison, where she has worked since 2013, and leverages research background in Biophysics and Life Sciences Communication.
